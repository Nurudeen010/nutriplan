Frequently asked questions

Is NutriPlan free to use?
Yes — completely free. No sign-up, no credit card, no subscription. Just open the app and generate your plan.
What Nigerian cuisines are supported?
Yoruba, Igbo, Hausa, Delta/Rivers, and a mixed All-Nigerian option. Each generates dishes authentic to that food culture.
How accurate are the Naira budget estimates?
Budget estimates are calibrated to 2026 Nigerian market prices using NBS data. They reflect realistic costs for home cooking across different income levels.
Can I use this for weight loss?
Yes. Select "Weight loss" as your health goal and the AI generates calorie-deficit Nigerian meals — without sacrificing the foods you love.
What if I don't like the generated plan?
Hit the Regenerate button — a completely new AI-generated plan is created instantly with the same preferences.
Is my data stored?
No. NutriPlan stores nothing. Your preferences are used only to generate your plan and are never saved or tracked.
